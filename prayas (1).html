The real problem

Generic AI fails students when clarity matters most.

01

It does not know your level

Generic AI answers the whole world. You need something that understands whether you need a simple explanation, a structured answer, or a quick revision push.

02

It does not know how you are being tested

A short answer, long answer, quiz, worksheet, or viva all need different support. Generic AI often ignores that and gives the wrong kind of help.

03

It treats every student the same

You might struggle with electricity. Your friend might struggle with writing. One generic answer cannot support both of you properly.
